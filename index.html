Stroll around Paris like the local you wish you were

My top recommendations for the traveller who refuses to be a tourist

What should I do in Paris?
Coucou friend! You recently asked me what to do on your upcoming trip to Paris, and since I have such a wide variety of suggestions for my friends to do while visiting the best city in the world (which coincidentally do not include heading up the Eiffel Tower or buying yourself a box of Laudurée macarons), here are a few of my ideas for what makes a visit to Paris amazing. Enjoy!

Walks & Explorations

Villas in the 19ième Arrondissement

A little bit of San Francisco in Paris - love the variety of houses in this neighborhood (down little streets called 'Villas') Métro: Danube

Montmartre

Especially down rue des Trois Frères for an unexpected old photobooth in the side of a building for an always-original Paris souvenir (4€) and around rue Lepic

Belleville

Stroll around especially for the colorful street art and hip, off-the-beaten-path cafes and restaurants. Parc de Belleville has great views and Moncoeur Belleville is great to prendre un verre (grab a drink).
